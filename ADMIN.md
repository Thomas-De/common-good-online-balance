## Setup

### Settings

In the ``ecg_balancing\settings.py``:

* ``FEEDBACK_INDICATOR_SENDER_EMAIL``
    * Value: email as String
    * The email from which email notifications will be sent out 

* ``COMPANY_ADMIN_EMAIL``
    * Value: email as String
    * receives emails to approve new companies
